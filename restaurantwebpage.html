WE ARE LEGEND

WE DON’T JUST MAKE PIZZA. WE MAKE PEOPLE’S DAYS. VINCENT
PIZZERIA WAS BUILT ON THE BELIEF THAT PIZZA SHOULD BE
SPECIAL, AND WE CARRY THAT BELIEF INTO EVERYTHING WE DO.
What’s our purpose at Vincent? Moments of Excitement. All made possible by
our flavor, innovation and, most importantly, by unlocking potential in our
team members.
With more than 50 years of experience under our belts, we understand how
to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles.
Instead of following trends, we set them. We create food we’re proud to
serve and deliver it fast, with a smile. No matter where you find us, we’re
making sure each meal our customers enjoy is delicious and one-of-a-kind.
Vincent was one of the first restaurants I discovered upon moving to New York
last summer, and it remains a favorite. Despite its sizable menu full of pastas,
sandwiches, and pizzas, I almost always get the same thing – the Vincent pizza.
It’s made with Ricotta & Marinara sauces, spiced with oregano, and topped
with eggplant, red onions, basil, Pecorino Romano & Mozzarella. It really is one
of the best pizzas I’ve ever had – and I eat a lot of pizza.

ADAM JEFFERSON

Lawyer, New York
